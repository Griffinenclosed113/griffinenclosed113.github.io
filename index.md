## 🔐 Data security practices

Follow these steps to keep your data safe.

1. Store backups on a separate drive from your main system.
2. Keep one backup copy in a separate building or at a different site.
3. Test your backups once a month. Try to restore a few files to check if the data remains readable.
4. Label your drives clearly so you know which contains your files.
